WhenCast Support

Welcome to WhenCast Support! Get help with weather-based activity planning and make the most of your outdoor time.

Quick Start Guide

Getting Started

  1. Download WhenCast from the App Store
  2. Allow location access when prompted for accurate weather data
  3. Toggle on activities you care about in the Alerts tab
  4. Check the Decisions tab for instant recommendations

Understanding Decisions

  • YES = Perfect conditions for your activity
  • WAIT = Conditions will improve soon
  • NO = Poor conditions, try another time

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: How accurate are the weather predictions?
A: WhenCast uses professional weather data sources. While we strive for accuracy, weather can be unpredictable. Always use your judgment for safety-critical activities.

Q: Why don't I see all activities?
A: Some activities require WhenCast Pro. Free users get access to Running, Car Wash, and BBQ activities.

Q: Can I use WhenCast without internet?
A: WhenCast requires internet connection to fetch current weather data for accurate recommendations.

Location & Settings

Q: How do I add multiple locations?
A: Go to the Locations tab → Tap the "+" button (Pro feature) → Search for your desired location → Tap to save it

Q: How do I change temperature units?
A: Go to Settings → tap Temperature Unit → choose Fahrenheit or Celsius

Q: Why is my location wrong?
A: Make sure location services are enabled for WhenCast in your device settings. You can also manually select a saved location in the Locations tab.

WhenCast Pro

Q: What's included in WhenCast Pro?
A: Unlimited saved locations, 15+ premium activity alerts, custom weather thresholds for each activity, extended forecast planning, and priority support.

Q: How do I restore my Pro purchase?
A: Go to Settings → tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore your subscription if you purchased on the same Apple ID.

Q: Can I cancel my subscription?
A: Yes, manage your subscription through your Apple ID settings: Open Settings app → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Find WhenCast → Cancel Subscription

Activities & Alerts

Q: How do I customize activity settings?
A: Pro users can customize settings by going to Settings → Decision Preferences → [Activity] Settings

Q: Why am I not getting notifications?
A: Check that notifications are enabled in device Settings → WhenCast, ensure the activity is toggled ON in the Alerts tab, and verify you have location access enabled.

Q: What activities does WhenCast support?
A: Free: Running, Car Wash, BBQ
Pro: Dog Walking, Painting, Plant Watering, Laundry Drying, Allergy Alerts, Rain Alerts, Lightning Alerts, Temperature Drop Alerts, Air Quality Alerts, Frost Warnings, Golden Hour Photography, and more!

Troubleshooting

App Issues

Problem: App crashes or freezes
Force close the app and restart → Restart your device → Update to the latest version of WhenCast → Contact support if issues persist

Problem: Weather data seems outdated
Pull down to refresh in the Decisions tab → Check your internet connection → Verify location services are enabled

Problem: Decisions seem inaccurate
Pro users can adjust thresholds in Settings → Remember recommendations are based on general conditions → Consider your personal preferences and local microclimates

Privacy & Safety

Your Privacy: WhenCast only uses location data for weather forecasts. No personal data is shared with third parties. All data processing follows Apple's privacy guidelines.

Safety Disclaimer: ⚠️ WhenCast provides general weather-based recommendations for informational purposes only. Always use your own judgment for safety-critical decisions. We are not responsible for any consequences resulting from app recommendations.
